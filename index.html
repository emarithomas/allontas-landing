Allontas

Allontas is a consumer financial planning and grocery comparison app designed to help users budget smarter, plan ahead, and make more informed spending decisions.

Our platform helps users plan for upcoming expenses, monitor household spending, and compare grocery prices across stores to better manage day-to-day finances.

We are currently in development and building a better way for consumers to proactively manage their money. Official website coming soon.
